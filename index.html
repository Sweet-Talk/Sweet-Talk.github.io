'Sweet Talk: The Achatic Rites' is the first chapter in a series of events and artworks by a new artist-led network formed by
Candice Jacobs & Erica Scourti.

'Sweet Talk: The Achatic Rites' links divinatory systems to the evolution of technology from ancient civilisation to the present moment, to explore the genuine potential for the personal and social transformation of an invited group of people with shared research concerns around the commodification of spiritual traditions, the construction of identity and the need to develop a new system of collective self-care.

Taking the startup conference trend of the ‘Fireside Chat’ at face value, Sweet Talk: The Achatic Rites will host a series of discussions, performances and social spaces for conversation around a campfire, a geodesic dome and pyramids in a outside location in Nottingham.
